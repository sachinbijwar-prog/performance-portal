# Smartavya Analytica — Employee Evaluation Guide 2026

Welcome to the **Smartavya Analytica Performance Portal**. This guide covers everything you need to complete your annual appraisal for the 2026 cycle.

---

## 1. Accessing the Portal

| Field | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **URL** | Internal Portal Link |
| **Username** | Your name-based ID — `firstname.lastname` (e.g., `sachin.bijwar`) |
| **Default Password** | `firstname@2026` (e.g., `sachin@2026`) |

> **First login?** Change your password immediately — see Section 5.

---

## 2. Navigating the Portal

When you log in, you land directly on your **KRA Appraisal** view. Use the **sidebar** (left panel) to switch between sections:

| Section | What it's for |
| :--- | :--- |
| **KRA Appraisal** | Rate yourself on Key Result Areas |
| **KSA Competency** | Rate yourself on core competencies |

The **header** (top bar) contains your key action buttons:

| Button | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| 🌙 / ☀️ (Moon/Sun icon) | Toggle Dark / Light Mode |
| **Export CSV** | Download your personal evaluation report |
| **Save Draft** | Save your progress to the cloud |
| **Submit Review** | Officially submit your self-evaluation |

Your **evaluation progress bar** is visible at the bottom of the sidebar — it tracks how much of your self-evaluation is complete.

---

## 3. Appraisal Sections

### 01. KRA Appraisal — Key Result Areas

Your performance is evaluated across **3 KRAs**, each with a defined weightage:

| # | KRA | Weightage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Technical Excellence & Architecture | 40% |
| 2 | Team Mentorship & Growth | 30% |
| 3 | Agile Delivery | 30% |

For each KRA:
- Select a **Self Rating** from 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest).
- Write a clear **Justification** — describe specific achievements, projects, or outcomes.
- Your L1 and L2 managers will add their ratings in the same card after you submit.

> The KRA section also includes **CoE Contributions** (Center of Excellence) — document any Knowledge Sharing sessions, tools/assets you built, or process improvements you drove. Add an evidence link (Confluence/Jira) where possible.

### 02. KSA Competency — Knowledge, Skills & Attributes

Rate yourself across **5 competency areas** on a scale of 1–5:

| # | Competency |
| :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Technical Skills |
| 2 | Problem Solving |
| 3 | Communication |
| 4 | Collaboration |
| 5 | Ownership |

Provide a brief justification for each rating.

### 03. Certifications

List any professional certifications earned during the 2026 cycle:
- Certification name
- Date of completion
- Link to digital credential (e.g., Credly, Coursera)

---

## 4. How Your Final Score is Calculated

Your final performance score is a weighted combination of KRA and KSA ratings:

```
Final Score = (KRA Weighted Score × 70%) + (KSA Average × 30%)
```

| Score Range | Rating Band |
| :--- | :--- |
| 4.50 – 5.00 | Outstanding (O) |
| 4.00 – 4.49 | Exceeds Expectations (EE) |
| 3.00 – 3.99 | Meets Expectations (ME) |
| 2.00 – 2.99 | Needs Improvement (NI) |
| Below 2.00 | Unsatisfactory (U) |

> The L2 Manager has final authority and may adjust ratings based on CoE contributions and certifications.

## 5. Changing Your Password

1. Open the **sidebar** (click the ☰ menu icon on mobile, or it's always visible on desktop).
2. Scroll to the **Account Security** section at the bottom of the sidebar.
3. Click **Change Password**.
4. Enter your new password (minimum 4 characters) and confirm it.
5. Click **UPDATE PASSWORD** — your new password is saved instantly to the cloud.

---

## 6. Dark Mode

Click the **🌙 moon icon** in the top-right header to switch to Dark Mode. Click the **☀️ sun icon** to switch back to Light Mode. Your preference is saved automatically and will persist across sessions.

---

## 7. Submission Workflow

The appraisal goes through 3 phases:

```
Phase 1: Self Evaluation  →  Phase 2: L1 Manager Review  →  Phase 3: L2 Finalization
```

1. Complete all KRA, KSA, CoE, and Certification sections.
2. Click **Submit Review** in the header — a confirmation dialog will appear.
3. Once submitted, your L1 Manager will be notified to begin their review.
4. After L1 approval, your L2 Manager provides the final sign-off.

> You can still **Save Draft** at any point before submitting. Your data is synced to the cloud in real time.

---

## 8. Exporting Your Report

Click the green **Export CSV** button in the top header at any time to download a personal copy of your evaluation. The export includes:
- Your self-ratings and justifications
- L1 and L2 manager ratings and comments
- Final KRA, KSA, and overall scores
